A comprehensive platform designed to help Ethiopian university students prepare for national exit exams through adaptive practice tests, collaborative study groups, and detailed progress analytics.
 
 ## üéØ Project Aim
The Problem
Ethiopian university students face significant challenges in preparing for national exit exams:

- Limited access to organized practice materials

- Lack of personalized learning paths

- Difficulty in finding study partners

- No centralized platform for exam preparation

## Our Solution
Ethio-Exit Prep Hub provides:

- Adaptive Practice Tests: AI-powered tests that adjust difficulty based on performance

- Collaborative Study Groups: Virtual study rooms for peer learning

- Progress Analytics: Detailed insights into strengths and weaknesses

- Resource Library: Curated materials specific to Ethiopian curriculum
